ABOUT ME

Ciao, I’m Stefano — a former software engineer turned full-time property care expert, based in the heart of Tuscany.

While living and working in the UK, I managed my Tuscan home from abroad — and quickly learned how challenging it can be to look after a property from a distance. That experience inspired me to start Good Neighbour Property Care: a service built on trust, flexibility, and a commitment to keeping homes safe, welcoming, and well looked after all year round.

Whether it’s stocking the fridge with your favourites, checking the power after a storm, collecting your mail, keeping your home clean, making small repairs, or taking care of ongoing maintenance — I aim to be the kind of local presence you’d want nearby. Like a good neighbour would.

If you think I could help, feel free to drop me an email. I’d love to chat — or even meet over coffee to talk about what you might need, big or small.
