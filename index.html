About Me

Hi, I’m Stefano — a former software engineer turned full-time property care expert based in beautiful Tuscany.

After over a decade working in the UK tech world while managing my own Tuscan home from afar, I realized how challenging it is to maintain a property remotely. That experience inspired me to create Good Neighbour Property Care — a service built around trust, flexibility, and the little details that make a house feel like home.

I’d love to hear from you — whether it’s to arrange services or just chat over coffee about how I can help take care of your home in Tuscany.
