About Me

I worked in the UK as a software engineer for over a decade, while owning a property back home in Tuscany. After a few years travelling back and forth, renovating and maintaining my house while living in another country became a challenge, and I realised that my desk job wasn’t what I wanted to do with my life. I decided to move back to Italy and put my property skills to good use — helping people like me, with second homes in the Tuscan hills, keep their properties safe, welcoming and secure all year round.

My main goal is to offer a wide range of on-demand services with a friendly, personal touch. Be it filling the fridge with your favourite mozzarella, checking up on your electricity after an epic storm or just collecting your post for you, I want to be a friendly face looking out for your property from afar, like a good neighbour would.

If you think I might be able to help you, just drop me an email. I’d love to speak to you, or even meet for coffee and a chat about whatever you might need, big or small.
